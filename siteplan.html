Overview

Site Name

CoffeShop

Domain Name: coffe-shop.com

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote a coffee shop in a local area. The idea is to showcase the products and the style of the shop to attract customers and drive engagement.

Scenarios

There are multiple scenarios that my website can address. One of the most common is: "How can I get in touch with the shop?" For this, I will create a form to collect and store customer information.

Another scenario is if my customers want to view the menu. I will create a dedicated page where they can see the menu and save it for later.
